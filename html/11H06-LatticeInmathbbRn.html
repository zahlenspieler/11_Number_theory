lattice in ℝn


Definition.

A lattice in Rn is an n-dimensional additive free group over Z which generates Rn over R.

Example: The following is an example of a lattice ℒ⊂ℝ2, generated by w1=(1,2),w2=(4,1).

ℒ={α⁢w1+β⁢w2∣α,β∈ℤ}
