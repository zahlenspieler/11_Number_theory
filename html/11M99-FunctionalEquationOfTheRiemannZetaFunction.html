functional equation of the Riemann zeta function


Let Γ denote the gamma function, ζ the Riemann zeta function and s any complex number. Then

πs-12⁢Γ⁢(1-s2)⁢ζ⁢(1-s)=π-s2⁢Γ⁢(s2)⁢ζ⁢(s).

Though the equation appears too intricate to be of any use, the inherent in the formula makes this the simplest method of evaluating ζ⁢(s) at points to the left of the critical strip.
