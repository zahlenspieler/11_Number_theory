Brocard’s problem


Brocard’s problem, first posed by Henri Brocard in 1876, asks for factorials that are one less than a square, that is, solutions to the equation n!+1=m2. Only three solutions are known: 4!+1=52, 5!+1=112 and 7!+1=712. Srinivasa Ramanujan also pondered the problem, in 1913. Erdős believed that there are no other solutions, and no more have been found for n up to 109.
