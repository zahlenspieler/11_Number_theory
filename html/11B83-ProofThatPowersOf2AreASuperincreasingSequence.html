proof that powers of 2 are a superincreasing sequence


We will prove a more general fact. If x≥2 then the sequence given by ak=xk is a superincreasing sequence.

Notice that a1=x>1=a0. Now we proceed by induction. We assume that

an=xn>∑j=0n-1aj=1+x+x2+x3+⋯+xn-1

We need to prove that an+1=xn+1>∑j=0naj. But

xn+1=x⋅xn≥2⁢xn=xn+xn>xn+(xn-1+xn-2⁢⋯+x+1)

so we conclude that

an+1>an+an-1+⋯+a1+a0

and so the sequence is superincreasing.
