exactly divides


Let a and b be integers and n a positive integer.  Then am exactly divides b (denoted as an∥n) if an divides b but an+1 does not divide b.  For example,  24∥48.

One can, of course, use the similar expression and notation for the elements a, b of any commutative ring or monoid (cf. e.g. divisor as factor of principal divisor).
