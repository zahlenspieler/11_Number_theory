Lindelöf hypothesis


Ernst Lindelöf (http://planetmath.org/ernstlindelof) presented in 1908 a conjecture known as Lindelöf hypothesis which concerns the of the Riemann zeta function on the “critical line”  x=12.  Up to now (2015), this hypothesis has not been proved.  It is weaker than the Riemann hypothesis such that this latter implies it but not conversely.

Lindelöf hypothesis.  ζ⁢(12+i⁢t)=O⁢(tε)  for every  ε>0  when  t→∞.

Here O is the Landau big ordo (http://planetmath.org/formaldefinitionoflandaunotation) notation.
