content of a polynomial


Let P=a0+a1⁢x+…+an⁢xn∈ℤ⁢[x] be a polynomial with integer coefficients. The content of P is the greatest common divisor of the coefficients of P.

c⁢(P)=gcd⁡(a0,a1,…,an)
