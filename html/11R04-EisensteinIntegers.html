Eisenstein integers


Let ρ=(-1+-3)/2, where we arbitrarily choose -3 to be either of the complex numbers whose square is -3. Note that ρ3=1. The Eisenstein integers are the ring ℤ⁢[ρ]={a+b⁢ρ:a,b∈ℤ}.
