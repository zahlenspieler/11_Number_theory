square


The square of a number x is the number obtained multiplying x by itself.  It’s denoted as x2.

Some examples:

52 = 25
(13)2 = 19
02 = 0
(-0.5)2 = 0.25

A perfect square is the square of an integer. The first few perfect squares are 0, 1, 4, 9, 16, 25, ….
