Mertens’ first theorem


For any real number x≥2 we have

∑p≤xln⁡pp=ln⁡x+O⁢(1)

for all prime integers p.

Moreover, the term O⁢(1) arising in this formula lies in the open interval (-1-ln⁡4,ln⁡4).
