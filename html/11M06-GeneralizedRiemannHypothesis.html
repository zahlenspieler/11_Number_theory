generalized Riemann hypothesis


This generalization of the Riemann hypothesis to arbitrary Dedekind zeta functions that for any number field K, the only zeroes s of the Dedekind zeta function ζK⁢(s) that lie in the strip 0≤ℜ⁡s≤1 ℜ⁡s=12.
