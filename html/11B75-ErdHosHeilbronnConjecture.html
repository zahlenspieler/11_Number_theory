Erdős-Heilbronn conjecture


Let A⊂ℤp be a set of residues modulo p, and let h be a positive integer, then

h∧⁢A={a1+a2+⋯+ah∣a1,a2,…,ah⁢ are distinct elements of ⁢A}

has cardinality at least min⁡(p,h⁢k-h2+1). This was conjectured by Erdős and Heilbronn in 1964[1]. The first proof was given by Dias da Silva and Hamidoune in 1994.
